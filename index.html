Welcome to the Jones Lab

We are a research group at New Mexico Tech focused on geomicrobiology and biogeochemistry. Our work explores the intersection of microbial life and Earth processes, with particular emphasis on microbial sulfur cycling and microbe-mineral interactions in cave systems.

Research Focus

Our lab investigates microbial processes across diverse environments including cave systems, mine waste, marine sediments, and wetland ecosystems. We employ a combination of molecular biology, geochemistry, and field-based approaches to understand how microorganisms shape and are shaped by their geochemical environment.

Dr. Jones also serves as the Academic Director of the National Cave and Karst Research Institute (NCKRI), where we advance understanding of cave and karst systems through research and education.
